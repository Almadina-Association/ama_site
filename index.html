Our Story

A masjid built
by seven families,
now home to many.

In 2010, we set out on an inspiring mission. With just seven families, we aimed to create a masjid where our community could unite, worship, and grow together. Today, we serve daily five-times prayer, host youth programs, and lay the foundation for a full-time Islamic school.
