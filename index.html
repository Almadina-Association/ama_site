About Us

Our Story

In 2010, we set out on an inspiring mission. With just 7 families, we aimed to create a Masjid where our community could unite, worship, and grow together.

Our Mission

To create a nurturing environment where our community can unite in faith, provide youth with Islamic education, strengthen family and community bonds, and build a foundation for a full-time Islamic school, all while offering daily five times prayer under the guidance of a learned Imam.
