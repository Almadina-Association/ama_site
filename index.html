About Us

Welcome to our Muslim community center located in Rosemount, MN. Our center is dedicated to providing a welcoming space for Muslims to offer daily prayers and connect with their faith. We strive to create an Islamic environment that fosters a sense of community among the Muslim population in and around Rosemount.

We are committed to promoting a deeper understanding of Islam and its religious, social, ethical, and moral standards through various educational programs and events. Our center also offers opportunities to engage in Islamic education, cultural, social, and charitable activities that benefit both Muslims and non-Muslims in the community.

In addition, we are dedicated to pursuing mutual respect, understanding, and cooperation with other religious organizations. We believe in developing fraternal relations with non-Muslim communities and promoting interfaith dialogue to build stronger bonds and foster harmony among diverse communities.

At our community center, we are proud to provide a place where Muslims can connect with their faith, engage with the community, and contribute to the betterment of society.
