Get Athan+ by Masjidal

Follow these simple steps to download Athan+ and connect your phone to Al-Madina Association's live prayer times.

1

Download Athan+ App

Search for "Athan+" by Masjidal in the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play (Android), then download and install it.

App Store Google Play
2

Search for Al-Madina

Once the app is open, use the masjid search feature to find "Al-Madina" and add it as your favorite masjid. This will sync our exact prayer and Iqamah times directly to your phone.

3

View Accurate Prayer Times

Athan+ will display Al-Madina's correct Salat and Iqamah timings. You can also see the prayer schedule for the next 7 days — all updated directly by the masjid.

Enable notifications in the app to receive prayer reminders automatically — never miss a prayer again!
